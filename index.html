Ques1 :- Explain the structure of heart and a well-labbeled diagram of heart.

Ans :- Heart is a muscular organ divided into four chambers: two atria (right and left) and two ventricles (right and left). These chambers are separated by valves (tricuspid, pulmonary, mitral, and aortic) that ensure one-way blood flow. The heart wall has three layers: epicardium, myocardium, and endocardium. Major blood vessels like the aorta, vena cava, and pulmonary arteries and veins connect the heart to the rest of the body.

Ques2 :- Why "ATP" is also known as energy currency of cell?Draw its flowchart.

Ans2 :- ATP (adenosine triphosphate) is known as the "energy currency" of the cell because it is the primary molecule used by cells to store and transfer energy for various cellular processes. Just like money is used to facilitate transactions in an economy, ATP facilitates energy transfer within the cell.
